About Us

From humble beginnings in the late '90s, we’ve grown into something we never imagined. What started as a group of friends creating music has evolved into a global journey, fueled by the power of connection. Every song, every album, is our way of sharing our story—and celebrating yours. We are Coldplay, a band that started as a simple idea in the minds of four friends—Chris, Jonny, Guy, and Will. From the first notes played in small rehearsal rooms to the vast stadiums where we perform today, every step of our journey has been filled with passion, dreams, and the deep desire to connect with you through music. Over the years, we've poured our hearts into every song, every album, hoping that in some small way, we could help you feel something bigger than ourselves. Whether it's through the soaring notes of "Fix You," the energy of "Viva La Vida," or the introspective lyrics of "The Scientist," we’ve always sought to create something that resonates, that lasts. Thank you for being part of this journey. We’re more than just a band—we're a community, and you are an essential part of that.
